
# Instructions

This game is a Choose Your Own Adventure game, called Stuck In the Terminal. 

In this game, you as the player must make decisions by entering 1,2 and sometimes 3 in the keyboard then clicking enter to lock in their choice.  

After each choice there is new storytelling, a game, the player escaping or the player losing the game.  

The instructions will be split up into sections for different parts of the story 

 
The Beginning 

Type python Game.py into the terminal to start the game 

Read through the printed lines, and wait until the end (when the dots take up a line) 

Once all the choices are shown on screen, make your choice by typing one of the numbers from the options given. (Make sure to not put a space before or after, and put a valid number) 

Continue this for any more dialogue + choices 

 

Robot Fight Game 

Certain story selections lead you to a robot fight game, this is how you play 

Read the story and make sure you understand how to attack, and what the shots do, as a little review: 

Basic Shot: Two random numbers (1-6) multiplied by each other 

Poison Shot: Four Random numbers (1-6), a, b, c, and d. These numbers  are added as such: a*c + b*d but are capped at 15 damage. The left-over damage is added to a poison damage counter. This counter will do a fifth of its damage each round 

Chance Shot: A 1 in 15 chance to just win the fight right then and there, but if you lose, you are out of the game 

The game will start, and you will be given the option to perform a basic, poison, or chance shot.  

Choose accordingly with the number associations, and take your shot 

Make sure you think about your strategy before taking your shots, maybe you can stack poison or just go for all basic 

You keep going until wither you win, or the robot kills you 

Goodluck! 

 

 
Light Projectile Game 

Certain story selections lead you to a light projectile game, this is how you play 

Very simple game where the player enters the angle to shoot a light ray to try and get it the farthest 

This game assumes a light ray speed of 10m/s (yes, a lot less than actual light ray) and a gravity acceleration of 9.8m/s^2. 

You have 5 attempts to try and get the highest distance of x and y 

Your highest point of the throw + your range is your total score 

If you obtain a distance of 13+ you get 5 points and move on 

 

House of Wisdom Game 

Certain story selections lead you to the House of Wisdom game, this is how you play 

Master Jeff will give you quotes, and you must find the meaning behind the quotes 

There will be the quote then three possible options for you to choose from, think hard and don't worry if you don't get all of them right 

You need to correctly analyse 3 out of the 5 to win and escape the terminal 

 

Finish the Sentence Game 

Certain story selections lead you to a Finish the Sentence game, this is how you play 

You will get some overview by Master Jeff, but he will give you some sentences 

These sentences will have a blank, and some condition to meet 

Such as (don't use the letters l m n o or p) 

You must try to fill in the blank with following the conditions, and try to make it make sense 

These sentences were made to help the player gain deeper meaning 

If you get to this game, you get an instant win, just play through and you have escaped! 

 

 
Trivia/Riddles Game 

Certain story selections lead you to a Trivia/Riddles game, this is how you play 

This game is all about answering Master Jeffâ€™s Trivia and riddles 

Don't worry, they are not super hard, and more made to teach the player a little bit of hidden meaning 

You will get three in total (a combination of trivia and riddles), and you must get 2 out of the 3 correct to win the game 

There are 3 options for each riddle/trivia question 

Just take your time and think! 

Meet People Game 

Certain story selections lead you to the meet people game, this is how you play 

In this game you are just there to see who you vibe with 

You will meet 7 different individuals, each with different personalities 

You have to choose which person(s) you think you could be friends with 

There is not much of a way to lose this game, just a lesson to be learnt!  

Follow the prompts in the terminal and keep answering different people if it asks you who else. 

 

General Story Instructions 

Make sure you read every line, whether it helps with the story, or gives you some nice wisdom, they all serve a purpose 

You can take as much time as you need, scroll up even, and not speed through the game! 

Try to play the game with a very open mind, Master Jeff and the Storyteller have a lot of quotes, and lessons thrown at the player throughout the game 

Have Fun and Escape the Terminal!
